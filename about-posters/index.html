Poster Printing Information for DEBS 2025 Participants

In addition to the accepted Posters and Demos, all accepted contributions to the Research and Industry tracks are also invited to bring a poster to the conference. This offers a great opportunity to foster additional discussions during the poster session. The preferred poster size is A1, but A0 is also possible.

If you prefer to print your poster on-site rather than bringing it with you, Chalmers printing services are available. There are two ways to proceed:

  • Option 1: Email Submission and On-Site Payment
    Send your poster as a PDF file to chalmersdigitaltryck@chalmers.se. Please indicate in the email if you prefer the poster to be printed in A1 size; otherwise, the default size will be A0.
    Once your poster is ready, you will receive a confirmation reply. You can then collect it in person and pay on-site at the printing service.
    Important: Please send the email at least two working days before you plan to collect the poster, and CC the General Chairs, Vincenzo Gulisano and Marina Papatriantafilou.
  • Option 2: Walk-in Printing
    Bring your poster on a USB stick, and visit the printing service directly to print and pay on-site.
